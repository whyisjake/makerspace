What is Mentor?

We aim to build literacy in design, science, technology, engineering, art, and mathematics, by combining what O'Reilly Media, MAKE Magazine and Otherlab have learned about the maker community. We wish to engage as much as possible with the broader maker community, leveraging the fantastic energy and talents of everyone doing beautiful things.

Our emphasis is threefold:

  1. Self-directed learning (building your own project as a better motivator to engage in engineering).
  2. Lower the cost of building & realizing dream projects through lower cost tools (software and hardware).
  3. Making making more social and engaging.
